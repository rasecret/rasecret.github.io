ORBITCE

ORBITCE is a national level technical symposium.
This year with loaded stuff.

A National Level Technical Symposium organized by the Association of Electronics & Communication Engineers from SSN College of Engineering.

Right from its birth on 2006, ORBITCE has always proven to be the talk of the town.

The daylong extravaganza with so many prizes is the main thing to be considered. Thousands of students throng to this technical meet every year. Orbitce ‘11 is this year’s edition of the event and promises a huge total prize money of about Rs.1,00,000/-. Orbitce ‘11, to be held on August 26th, is definitely the place to be for students who want to prove their mettle.

This year we are back with a pandora of events including Paper Presentation, Project Display, Mathomania, Freeze Quiz, etc. which includes online technical quiz and gaming also. Be there in SSN on August 26, to prove your geekiness.
